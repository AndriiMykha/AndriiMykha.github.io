My Arriving

Arriving in Budapest felt surreal after enduring 15 hour s at the plane with the following 22-hour bus journey, leaving me completely exhausted. The city greeted me warmly through the kindness of strangers who assisted me with arranging a taxi. Despite the fatigue, the thought of finally being in Budapest filled me with excitement and anticipation. The initial encounter with the city's welcoming atmosphere reassured me that my journey was worth every moment of discomfort. As I settled in, the allure of Budapest began to unfold before me, promising unforgettable experiences ahead.

Wonderful people I met

Erasmus isn't just about hitting the books; it's about connecting with people from all walks of life. During my time there, I had the privilege of meeting some truly remarkable individuals. The diversity was astounding – people from every corner of the globe coming together under one roof. The interactions I had with them were nothing short of magical; each conversation, each shared experience enriched my understanding of the world and its myriad cultures. In those moments, I felt truly alive, surrounded by a tapestry of stories and perspectives that expanded my horizons in ways I never imagined possible.

Erasmus, for me, was about forging bonds that transcended borders, languages, and backgrounds – it was about celebrating the beautiful tapestry of humanity that makes our world so wonderfully diverse. From late-night discussions in cozy cafes to impromptu adventures exploring the city's hidden gems, every moment spent with my fellow Erasmus companions was a treasure. Together, we laughed, we learned, and we grew – not just as students, but as global citizens united by our shared experiences and our shared humanity. And though our time together may have been fleeting, the memories we created and the friendships we formed will stay with me for a lifetime. Erasmus wasn't just an educational experience; it was a journey of the heart, a journey that opened my eyes to the beauty of our world and the boundless potential of human connection.

Everything that happened with me

During my Erasmus adventure, I was thrilled to discover a myriad of new experiences awaiting me at every corner. The array of events, from cultural festivals to academic seminars, offered me a taste of the vibrant tapestry of activities that enriched the city's atmosphere. As I navigated through the bustling streets, I found myself tantalized by the diverse array of foods, each dish a testament to the city's culinary diversity and rich gastronomic heritage. Beyond the culinary delights, I immersed myself in the intricate fabric of the local culture, eagerly embracing customs and traditions that expanded my worldview and deepened my appreciation for diversity. Alongside the exploration of new foods and cultures, I found joy in discovering and cultivating new hobbies, from salsa dancing to photography, each adding a colorful dimension to my Erasmus journey. In every moment, I was met with the thrill of the unknown, eager to unravel the mysteries and treasures that awaited me in this captivating city. Erasmus became not just a chapter in my academic journey, but a transformative experience that broadened my horizons and enriched my soul with the beauty of discovery and exploration.
